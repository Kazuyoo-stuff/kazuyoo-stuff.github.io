About Project
rocket_launch What is Celestial Lab?

Celestial Lab is where Android optimization experiments, tweaks, and lightweight modules are born. Every project is built around three pillars: efficiency, stability, and control.

All projects and tools here are independently developed to improve performance and user experience across various Android devices.
